We Believe Travel is Better Together

TripSquad was born from late night group chats, endless spreadsheets, and the chaos of planning trips with friends.

Our Story

We've all been there, trying to coordinate a group trip only to end up through scattered messages, forgotten details, and that one friend who never responds. In 2024, We decided there had to be a better way. TripSquard started as simple idea: What if planning a group trip could be as exciting as the trip itself? Today, TripSquad helps thousands of travelers turn travel dreams into reality, one adventure at a time.

Our Mission

To make group travel planning effortless, collaborative, and fun. We believe that the best memories are made with the people you love.

Our mission is to remove the stress from trip planning so you can focus on what matters the adventure ahead and the people you're sharing it with.

Our Values

Community First

Travel brings people together. We build tools that strengthen friendships and create lasting bonds.

Simplicity

Planning shouldn't be complicated. We design with clarity and ease in mind.

Curiosity

Innovation

What We Do

TripSquad is your all-in-one group travel planning platform.
From brainstorming destinations to splitting costs, we handle the logistics so you can handle the fun.


We help you:

  • Collaborate with travel squard in real-time
  • Organize itineraires, bookings, and budgets in one place.
  • Discover destinations that match your group's vibe.
  • Stay connected before, during, and after your trip.
